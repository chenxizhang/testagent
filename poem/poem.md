# The Quiet Hour

Between the hush of dusk and dawn,
The stars emerge one by one,
Each light a whisper, softly drawn
Across the canvas of the sun.

The wind moves gently through the trees,
A melody without a name,
And in that stillness, by degrees,
The world and dreaming feel the same.

Hold close the moments in between,
The breath before the story's told,
For every quiet thing unseen
Is worth far more than words of gold.
